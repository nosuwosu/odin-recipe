Kimchi Fried Rice

Do you want an easy fried rice meal from left overs? This is the recipe you're looking for. Make a complete meal in less than 20 minutes. This is kimchi fried rice which is a korean dish has become popular in recent time specially among college students.

Ingredients

  • 2 bowls of rice (preferred if a day old)
  • A cup of kimchi (or more depending on how you like it) and should be aged
  • However much kimchi juice you like
  • 2 table spoons of gochujang (red chili paste)
  • 3 teaspoons of sesame oil
  • 3 eggs
  • chopped green onions
  • Seaweed strips
  • 2 sticks of butter
  • Your choice of protein

Directions

  1. Heat up pan and add butter.
  2. Add rice, kimchi and the egg.
  3. add the kimchi juice, water, and gochujang.
  4. Stir all the ingredients together until orange colour.
  5. Let the meat cook thoroughly.
  6. Add sesame oil and shortly turn off heat after.
  7. Finally add the green onions and seaweed on top.
  8. Serve while it's hot for best taste.
