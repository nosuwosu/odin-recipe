Cookies n' Cream Fudge

Do you crave sweets often? Here's a recipe for your sweet tooth for home or to go. You can make many of these as snacks within an hour. It has a delight creamy texture and very soft and chewy. This recipe is good for 25 pieces or more however you want to divide them up.

Ingredients

  • 18 oz white chocolate (510g)
  • 14 oz of sweetened condense milk (400g)
  • 12 sandwiched cookies(oreo), chopped

Directions

  1. In a mixing bowl combine chocolate with sweetened condensed milk and melt in the microwave in 15-second increments.
  2. When the chocolate is fully melted, add the chopped cookies and stir until combined.
  3. Pour the mixture into a parchment paper-lined baking tin.
  4. Put in the fridge or freezer until the fudge is solid.
  5. Cut into bite-sized pieces and Enjoy!
