Potato Wedges

Ultra crispy oven baked potato wedges cooked to golden brown perfection. These are the perfect side dish for any meal. You can never go wrong with these.

Ingredients

  • 3 large russet potatoes
  • 1/3 cup of olive oil (80 mL)
  • 2 teaspoons of salt
  • 1 teaspoon of paprika
  • 1/2 teaspoon of pepper
  • 1 table spoon of garlic powder
  • 2 tablespoons of fresh parsley
  • cooking spray
  • 1/3 cup of grated parmesan cheese

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 400 degrees fahrenheit or 200 Celsius.
  2. Wash potatoes and cut into 8ths.
  3. Toss the wedges in olive oil and the seasoning.
  4. Place wedges on baking sheet evenly.
  5. Bake for 40 min for a crispy finish!
